About Afro Divine Stock Limited

Our Background

Afro Divine Stock Limited was founded by Mr. Mugarura Katuufu in 2020.It was licensed in 2023 after meeting all the required standards for its operations.
At first, it operated on a small scale and later in 2024,it expanded due to foreign investments.

Mission

Our mission is to provide traceable, high quality produce to international buyers while supporting local communities with training and fair procurement.
Also to connect African farmers to global markets, promoting sustainable and ethical trade practices.
We take pride in our expertise in sourcing and exporting a diverse range of food products.

Vision

We are looking forward to become a leading player in the global food export industry.
Our commitment to quality,sustainability and customer satisfaction has enabled us to build a strong reputation in the global market.
We strive to continuously improve our services and product offerings to meet the evolving needs of our customers.
